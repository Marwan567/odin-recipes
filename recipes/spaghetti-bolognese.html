Spaghetti Bolognese

What is Spaghetti Bolgonese?

Spaghetti Bolognese is a popular Italian dish made from a hearty meat sauce and spaghetti pasta. The sauce is usually made from ground beef or a mixture of ground beef and other meats, simmered with onions, garlic, canned tomatoes, and other seasonings. The sauce is typically seasoned with salt, black pepper, dried basil, and oregano, and is served over spaghetti pasta. Some variations may also include carrots, celery, or mushrooms. Spaghetti Bolognese is a comfort food staple and is enjoyed by many around the world, including those following a Halal diet.

Spaghetti Bolognese ingredients

  • 500g spaghetti pasta
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups canned tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Dried basil and oregano, to taste
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

How to cook Spaghetti Bolognese

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef to the saucepan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon.
  5. Stir in the canned tomato sauce, salt, black pepper, dried basil, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti pasta and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese, if desired.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Bolognese!
