Lasagna

Lasagna Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made of wide, flat noodles layered with a mixture of meat or vegetables, cheese, and a rich tomato sauce. This dish is often baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbly, resulting in a warm and comforting meal. Lasagna is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit individual tastes and can be made ahead of time for easy meal preparation. It is a classic dish that is loved by many and continues to be a staple in homes and restaurants around the world.

Lasagna ingredients

  • 450 grams of oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 450 grams of ground beef, sausage, or a mixture of both
  • 1.4 liters of tomato sauce
  • 425 grams of ricotta cheese
  • 227 grams of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 120 milliliters of grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 5 milliliters of salt
  • 2.5 milliliters of black pepper
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, chopped

      • Cooking steps

      1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
      2. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain the fat and set aside.
      3. In the same saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
      4. Stir in 3 cups of tomato sauce, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
      5. In a separate bowl, mix together 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 2 eggs (if using), 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese, and a handful of chopped fresh basil leaves.
      6. Spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce mixture on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
      7. Place a layer of oven-ready lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, breaking the noodles if necessary to fit.
      8. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat the layering process, ending with a layer of tomato sauce on top.
      9. Sprinkle 8 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese over the top.
      10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbly.
      11. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
